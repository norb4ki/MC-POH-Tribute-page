MC POH

The man who saved russian hip-hop music

Here is timeline of MC Poh life:

  • 1996 - Born in Saint-Petersburg.
  • 2003 - In a preschool, graduated with honors.
  • 2008 - Wrote and published his first song "Vesenniy les", which claimed #1 place in all charts for entire year.
  • 2010 - Wrote and published "Banka-parilka" and "Shkola".
  • 2012 - Became the first ever russian "Hip-Hop Hall of Fame" member in New-York.
  • 2014 - Made a feat with AC/DC.
  • 2015 - Ended his career and disappeared from the media space.
